\subsection*{Rules}
\begin{markdown}
Place 10 stars in the puzzle: 2 per row, and 1 per column. Stars may not touch, even diagonally. Clues give the number of stars which are placed within the 3x3 box of cells centered at the number.
\end{markdown}
