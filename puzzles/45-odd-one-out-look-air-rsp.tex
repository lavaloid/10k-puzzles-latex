\subsection*{Rules}
\begin{markdown}
Normal Look-Air rules apply, *except*



that **exactly one** square is not a square! Instead, it is a 1xN "line", where N > 1. The line additionally cannot see any 1x1s, or any NxNs.
\end{markdown}
